WarehouseX

The Problem

Warehouses and smaller businesses rely on manual systems for tracking their inventory, processing orders, and monitoring stock. this usually leads to inaccuracies, delays, and all-round increased risk.

Features

  • Role-based access for administrators, warehouse managers, and staff
  • Inbound and outbound inventory tracking
  • stock level updates with low stock alerts
  • analytics and reports for inventory valuation

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, javascrypt (frontend)
  • FastAPI / Node.js (backend)
  • PostgreSQL (database)
  • RESTful APIs
