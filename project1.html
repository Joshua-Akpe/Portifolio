Smart Campus Feedback and Management System

The Problem

In many institutions, students and staff lack a centralized, transparent, and efficient way to report issues and provide feedback about campus facilities and services. Existing methods are typically informal, slow or inaccessible, which leads to unresolved issues and poor communication.

Features

  • Role-based access for students, staff and administrators
  • categorized feedback with optional anonymity
  • real-time feedback tracking and analytics
  • automated notifications and reminders
  • responsive design for mobile and desktop

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, Javascript (Frontend)
  • FastAPI (Backend)
  • PostgreSQL (Database)
  • RESTful APIs
